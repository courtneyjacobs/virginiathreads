Welcome to the Virginia Threads Project!

About Us

The Virginia Threads Project is a student-run organization at the University of Virginia and was founded in the summer of 2020. We are a diverse group of undergraduates from all four years who are pursuing many different areas of study.

Why We Created This Project

We designed this project because we wanted to support early career faculty hires during future crises. These young professionals are the most diverse group in academia and reflect an increasingly more balanced gender distribution among faculty. However, they are most often subject to direct impacts in disasters, such as hiring freezes or hiring reductions. We wanted to give back to them after all they do for us, so they can continue doing what they love.
