Welcome to the Virginia Threads Project!


We are UVA students who create high-quality, timeless knitwear that anyone can buy and love. As a socially responsible business, the majority of our profits go to the UVA Emergency Hiring Fund, or EHF. The EHF supports early-career faculty hiring at the University of Virginia in times of need, including pandemics, economic recessions, and natural disasters.
